# true-contracts
### Introduction
TrueMarkets is a decentralized prediction market that enables users to forecast the outcomes of real-world events. It aims to break down information asymmetries pertaining to global news allowing people to express real-time sentiment around the odds of potential outcomes.

While traditional news sources propagate the opinions of key leaders, TrueMarkets propagates news through the power of open markets.

Although the subjective sentiment expressed through a market does not bear any truth itself, it incentivizes the search for truth through its resolution. 

These event-based markets are essentially “questions” created using a set of transparent guidelines and resolved by the app’s enshrined oracle based on their respective resolution rules.

By allowing people to forecast the outcomes of global events, TrueMarkets fosters the search for truth.

If a prediction market is a truth machine, then its “truth” is only as good as its source (oracle). 

Asking a question is easy, but finding the answer is hard. Every question has an answer, but that answer has a cost. 

Truemarkets provides bounties for truth.
